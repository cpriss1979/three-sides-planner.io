About the Themes

Bingo Yellow

Inspired by a game Brenton and I play with yellow cars. It’s joyful and playful, just like our bond.

Bumblebee Yellow

Brenton’s childhood nickname was “Bumblebee.” This theme celebrates his energy, resilience, and the love between a mom and son.

Stella Peach

A tribute to Carlie, who welcomed me with kindness and gave me the sacred gift of time with Delilah. This theme represents new beginnings, trust, and warmth.

Santafe Red

For Rick. He’s always loved trains—especially the Santa Fe line—and red has always been his favorite color. This theme reflects his passion, creativity, and the unique joy he brings into our lives. Just like his hobbies, it’s full of motion, nostalgia, and color—and just like Rick, it stands out in the best way.

Dolphin Blue

This theme is named for Bre. From a special vacation with her and Brenton, where we swam with dolphins at the Indianapolis Zoo. It’s calm, joyful, and full of shared memories.

Moonlight Blue

Named after Luna. We don’t see her often, but when we do, it brings real joy and happiness. This soft blue reflects those moments—bright, peaceful, and deeply meaningful.

JR Green

Named after my brother JR. A strong, classic green that honors our bond and the steady foundation he represents.

Monkey Green

Braden’s dad used to call him “Monkey,” and now he uses that nickname for the people he loves. It’s playful, affectionate, and full of love.

Golf Green

For my dad, who i used to golf with when i was a teenager. He who now golfs almost every day in his retirement. This grounded green reflects his calm spirit, steady habits, and how deeply he values his routines. He’s always been consistent and dependable, and this color is a quiet tribute to the peace and strength he brings me.

Strawberry Pink

Named after my granddaughter, who has a world full of strawberry-themed everything. This color is sweet, joyful, and bursting with her bright little spirit. Babysitting her has been one of the greatest joys of my life—a beautiful reminder of innocence, giggles, and unconditional love. This theme is for her—for all the sunshine she brings, and the way she’s filled my heart in ways only a granddaughter can.

Shortcake Pink

For Everly. I gave her my Strawberry Shortcake baby blanket from when I was little. Though I haven’t gotten to have a relationship with her, she’s always in my heart.

Prissy Pink

My signature color—feminine, strong, and soft. This theme represents self-love and embracing who I am fully.

Date Rose

Rick was the first man to ever give me flowers—and he still surprises me with them sometimes. This theme is for him and the love we’ve built together.

Sunburst Purple

For my moms favorite color, and a memory shared about a Sunburst Beauty Pageant rhinestone pin that meant the world to me, being stolen from our hotel room at one of my other pageants. It’s more than just purple—it’s about her love and support I have always felt from her.

Bubby-Bear Brown

Braden’s nickname is “Bubby.” I added “Bear” to reflect his strength, protectiveness, and quiet warmth. This theme feels earthy and deeply loving—just like him.

Brady Blue

Inspired by the Brady Bunch. Rick and I have always said our family is like theirs—blended, chosen, and built with love. This theme is for everyone in our life who became family, whether by blood or by heart.

Original Theme

The very first design before the colors had names. It’s where everything started—a simple beginning that led to something full of meaning.
